Meridian

A desk object with no purpose but to be held — machined from a single billet of grade-5 titanium, bead-blasted, then hand-finished over three days.

[ Studio ]

Most products are drawn, then made. We do it the other way — we machine first, holding the metal until the form tells us what it wants to be.

Practice

Objects, tools, instruments and the occasional impractical sculpture. Small runs, real materials.

Method

CNC + hand. Every piece is finished by a person and carries its own number.

Materials

Titanium, tool steel, brass, anodised aluminium. Nothing plated, nothing painted.

Form, held to a thousandth.
